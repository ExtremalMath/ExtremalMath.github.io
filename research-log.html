Proof of Critical Density for Triangles (Corollary 3)
July 08, 2026

Finalized the proof for the critical density $d_3(K_3)$. By analyzing the "Cyclic Triple" conditions ($\alpha\beta + \gamma > 1$, etc.), I demonstrated that the threshold is exactly the Golden Ratio $\tau \approx 0.618$.

Key Steps:

  • Lower Bound: Constructed a $C_5$ example where densities equal $\tau$, showing no triangle exists.
  • Upper Bound: Proved that if densities exceed $\tau$, the cyclic inequalities force a triangle to exist.

Convex Hull Argument & Theorem 3
July 05, 2026

Worked on the proof for Theorem 3 using Carathéodory's Theorem. The goal was to show that a minimal counter-example (a weighted tripartite graph with no triangle but satisfying cyclic inequalities) cannot exist.

By reducing the vertex weights and applying convex combination arguments, I proved that any such minimal counter-example must have at most 2 vertices per part, leading to a contradiction.
